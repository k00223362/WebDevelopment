    About KI

    KI private Ltd. is the building stage to beat the current market demands with respect to Laboratory Chemical Plants and environmental chemicals.We jumped into current to eliminate the environnmental damage caused by chemical industries.We have strong and dedicated team of young dedicated,Professional and experienced Engineers. It is among the new born industries in todays world which manufactures and supplies high quality, cost effective and specialist chemical formulations to industry. We develop environmental and chemical process aids and additives for a variety of industries, including Environmental and Petroleum, Water Treatment, Pharmaceutical and the Public Sector.

    Our products are manufactured at various plants across the globe. Our facilities include Four dedicated Manufacturing Plants, covered and uncovered storage areas and a number of bulk chemical storage tanks. We deliver our products throughout the World using our dedicated fleet of Hazchem compliant vehicles.

    Our Research and development Staff are highly experienced in the formulation of products to our customers specific requirements. These facilities allow us to develop products to individual customer specifications.

    KI Ltd. currently employs approximately 39 people who are all expertly trained in their fields. We excel in areas such as product development, technical services, engineering back-up and customer care.

    KI Ltd. is completely devoted to quality in every aspect of our organisation. We have been accredited with the ISO standards since 2013 and currently hold the ISO 9001:2008.

    Our Office is located at Lower Glanmire Road,Cork, (1 miles from Cork City)

    Our manufacturing is conducted at various plants across the globe, allowing us tailor our products to meet customers changing needs.
